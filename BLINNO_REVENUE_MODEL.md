# BLINNO Platform Revenue Model

## How BLINNO Generates Revenue

BLINNO operates as a multi-sided marketplace platform connecting creators, businesses, and consumers worldwide. The platform generates revenue through several streams:

---

## üí∞ Primary Revenue Streams

### 1. **Transaction Fees (Marketplace Commission)**

**Current Status**: Infrastructure ready, fee structure to be implemented

**How it works**:
- BLINNO takes a percentage commission on all marketplace transactions
- Applied to:
  - Physical product sales
  - Digital product sales
  - Service bookings
  - Performance bookings
  - Commissions (custom work)

**Recommended Fee Structure**:
- **Marketplace Sales**: 5-10% commission per transaction
- **Digital Products**: 5-8% commission
- **Service Bookings**: 8-12% commission
- **Commissions**: 10-15% commission (higher due to custom work management)

**Implementation**:
- Fee calculation happens at checkout
- Platform fee deducted before creator payout
- Transparent fee breakdown shown to sellers

---

### 2. **Subscription Fees (Platform Subscriptions)**

**Current Status**: Infrastructure ready, subscription tiers to be implemented

**How it works**:
- Creators pay monthly/annual subscription fees for premium features
- Different tiers with varying benefits

**Proposed Subscription Tiers**:

#### **Free Tier** (Basic)
- Basic profile
- Limited product listings (5-10 items)
- Standard marketplace access
- Basic analytics
- **Fee**: Free

#### **Creator Tier** (Standard)
- Unlimited product listings
- Advanced analytics dashboard
- Priority customer support
- Featured listings
- Custom branding
- **Fee**: $4-8 USD/month

#### **Professional Tier** (Premium)
- All Creator features
- Advanced marketing tools
- API access
- White-label options
- Dedicated account manager
- **Fee**: $12-20 USD/month

#### **Enterprise Tier** (Business)
- All Professional features
- Custom integrations
- Bulk operations
- Advanced reporting
- Multi-user accounts
- **Fee**: Custom pricing

---

### 3. **Payment Processing Fees**

**Current Status**: Integrated with Click Pesa

**How it works**:
- BLINNO may charge a small payment processing fee on top of Click Pesa fees
- Or absorb Click Pesa fees and charge creators a flat transaction fee
- Typical model: 2-3% + fixed fee per transaction

**Revenue Model Options**:
- **Option A**: Pass-through model (Click Pesa fees only, no additional platform fee)
- **Option B**: Bundled fee (Platform fee includes payment processing)
- **Option C**: Separate fees (Platform fee + payment processing fee)

---
