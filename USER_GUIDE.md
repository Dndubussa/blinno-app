## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. Visit [www.blinno.app](https://www.blinno.app)
2. Click on "Sign Up" in the top right corner
3. Choose your role (creator, seller, freelancer, etc.)
4. Fill in your details including:
   - First name
   - Middle name (optional)
   - Last name
   - Country
   - Phone number with country code
5. Verify your email address
6. Complete your profile setup

### Logging In

1. Visit [www.blinno.app](https://www.blinno.app)
2. Click on "Sign In" in the top right corner
3. Enter your email and password
4. If you have two-factor authentication enabled, enter the code from your authenticator app

## Marketplace

### Browsing Products

1. Navigate to the Marketplace section
2. Use filters to narrow down results:
   - Category
   - Location
   - Price range
   - Rating
3. Click on any product to view details

### Purchasing Products

1. Add items to your cart
2. Review your cart
3. Proceed to checkout
4. Enter shipping information
5. Select payment method
6. Confirm and pay

### Selling Products (For Sellers)

1. Go to your dashboard
2. Click "Add Product"
3. Fill in product details:
   - Title
   - Description
   - Price
   - Category
   - Images
   - Inventory
4. Set shipping options
5. Publish your product

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

1. **Login Problems**:

2. **Payment Issues**:
   - Verify payment information
   - Check account balance
   - Contact payment provider

3. **Technical Issues**:
   - Refresh the page
   - Clear browser cache
   - Try a different browser
   - Contact support
