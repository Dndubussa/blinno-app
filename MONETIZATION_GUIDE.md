2. **Choose monetization methods** - Select which revenue streams work for you
3. **Create your offerings** - Add digital products, subscription tiers, or services
4. **Promote your work** - Share your BLINNO profile to attract customers
5. **Track earnings** - Monitor your revenue through the dashboard

## Best Practices

### For Artists:
- Showcase your best work in your portfolio
- Offer multiple price points for digital products
- Create subscription tiers with clear benefits
- Respond quickly to commission requests

### For Content Creators:
- Provide value in subscription tiers
- Create evergreen digital products
- Engage with your audience regularly
- Offer exclusive content for subscribers

### For Actors:
- Keep your performance calendar updated
- Showcase your range in your portfolio
- Offer acting courses or tutorials
- Build a fan base through subscriptions
